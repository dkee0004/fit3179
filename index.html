Water Pollution in Europe

A visualization to observe any kind of relationship between water pollution in Europe and the tourists in it.

Choropleth Map

An overview of the water pollution using a map. Some of the smaller countries may have a better reading of mean pH values for a body of water, but when the readings are normalized to mean pH values per 100000 tourists, it can be shown that it is actually worse off than the bigger countries.

Slope Graph

A slope graph showing the changes in the median of pH values across bodies of water in countries in Europe between 2008 to 2009. Based on this visualization, although the changes to the pH values in a year are in the decimal points, a point 1 or point 2 change in the value is already considered drastic. Relating this to the other visualizations and the topic at hand, we can see how the population and the number of tourists within the country, along with their bad practices, can fluctuate water pollution levels.
